About FlowMachinery

Engineering Excellence Since 1998

For over two decades, FlowMachinery has been at the forefront of industrial equipment innovation, delivering turnkey solutions that transform how the world manufactures.

Headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio, with regional offices in Munich, Shanghai, and Sao Paulo, we serve manufacturers across automotive, aerospace, energy, electronics, and heavy industry. Our team of 800+ engineers, technicians, and project managers brings deep domain expertise to every engagement, from single-machine installations to multi-site factory automation overhauls.
